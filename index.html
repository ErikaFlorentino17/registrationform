RULES OF FEES
TERMS OF PAYMENT
1. All fees are payable in cash upon enrollment or by installment basis. For the installment plan agreement are as follows:
a. Twenty-five percent (25%) of the total fees shall be collected upon enrollment.
b. Remaining seventy-five percent (75%) will be divided into each term one week before the examination.
2. Students who paid in cash basis are entitled for a "refund" or "withdraw" within a month (30 days) after the date of registration under the conditions:
a. 80% of the amount paid when he withdraws within the first week after the registrations, whether he has attended class or not.
b. 50% of the amount paid when he withdraws within the second, third, fourth week after the registration, whether he has attended class or not.
c. No refund will be made 30 days after registration.
